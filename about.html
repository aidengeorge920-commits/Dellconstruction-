About us

Built on trust, not shortcuts.

Our story

Six years of building it right

Dell Constructions was started with one goal: build homes the way we'd want built for our own family. Six years on, that hasn't changed.

That means being upfront about cost and timelines, working with tradespeople we trust, and staying involved from the first sketch to the day you get the keys. We only take on new home builds, so every project gets our full attention rather than being one of several types of job running at once.

What matters to us

Straight answers

No surprise costs. If something changes, you hear about it from us before it shows up on an invoice.

Real quality

We build to last, not just to pass inspection. Materials and trades are chosen on quality, not just price.

You're kept in the loop

Regular updates through the build so you always know exactly where things stand, even when plans change.
